About the Workshop

Empowering Women in Computing

WE-ICT 2026 provides a forum for researchers, faculty members, industry professionals, and students to exchange ideas, experience, and success stories to encourage, support, connect and motivate Bangladeshi women in computing.

Mission

Creating opportunities for women in ICT through knowledge sharing, mentorship, and networking in higher education and research.

Organizer

Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)

Project

Organized under subproject CP#2082: Enhancement of Graduate Studies Facilities for CSE, BUET under HEQEP

Location

Publication

Proceedings

WE-ICT will not have formal proceedings. However, a booklet (without ISBN number) of accepted posters, write-ups by Bangladeshi distinguished female ICT personnel, and statistics of female graduates, will be distributed at the workshop for the benefit of the community and must be regarded as a collection of preprints.

Publication Opportunities

Results presented at WE-ICT may be published in other conferences with formal proceedings and/or in journals. Posters of already accepted papers at other conferences can also be submitted at WE-ICT.

Call for Posters

Submit Your Research

We invite authors to submit 2-page extended abstracts on novel research ideas or results related to the application of ICT for the empowerment of women in different sectors.

Poster Session Highlights

The poster session aims to provide an opportunity to present and have an informal discussion of one's research with the participants of the workshop. Best three posters will be awarded.

Posters of already accepted papers at other conferences can also be submitted at WE-ICT. In addition, ideas and results presented at WE-ICT may be published in other conferences with formal proceedings and/or in journals.
